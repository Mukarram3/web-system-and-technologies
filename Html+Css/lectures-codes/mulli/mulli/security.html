Security and Encryption

Your security is our top priority. We protect your financial information using a three-point system with industry grade encryption. This provides the highest level of security, which protects credentials and sensitive data in the highly unlike scenario of a breach in our data vault.

How to access your users’ information

All our endpoints communicate using HTTPS and we utilize user-specific 256-bit AES keys and application specific RSA encoded public keys. Since your data requires access keys that are stored both on your user’s device and your application, not even Mulli has access to any decrypted user information.
